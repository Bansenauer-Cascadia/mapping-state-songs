    About Us

    As part of Cascadia’s Integrative Relay Project connected to the Maps Annual Integrated Learning Theme, over 275 students took up parts of the question of state songs, building upon each other’s work, passing different parts of the project from quarter to quarter, through 10 classes, and culminating in the State Tuned Phone App and Mixed Media Installation.

    The State Tuned mobile App provides the ability to listen to state songs, upload photos to facebook, geolocate your state, vote for a state song and like the overall application.

    We encourage you to also visit the State Tuned website.
